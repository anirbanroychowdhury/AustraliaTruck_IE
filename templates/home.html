Reason for this solution

Crashes caused by Fatigue

Within the Southern Region, fatigue crashes accounted for 11% of all crashes between 1996 and 1998. This is higher compared to drink driving crashes Fatigue crashes represent 11% of fatal and injury crashes.

Fatigue Crashes based on Age.

Driver age groups involved in fatigue related crashes. Age between 21-25 years has acquired around 35% and 40-49 years around 20% of the age groups. This descreases as we go above 49 years.
