Project Overview

Fatigued drivers are one of the leading causes of truck accidents in the state of Victoria. Most times the drivers are unaware of this illness and tend to drive longer with tiredness and eventually dozing off during their journey. Thus, the problem statement for the project is “Truck drivers, mainly when driving is mostly unaware of their sleep deprivation or tiredness. This lack of fatigue awareness caused by sleep apnea is causing drivers to doze off. This results in major truck accidents and is most common across the state”.

Solution

The proposed solution is to eradicate the fatigue problem which is the main cause of trucks accidents. AusTrcker aims to build a web application that serves truck drivers between the age of 21-25 and 40-49 who mainly suffer from fatigue due to sleep apnea or lack of physical activity. The application is equipped with an open CV which makes use of the device camera to check the drowsiness rate. Based on the count of blinking and the amount of time the eye lids were closed, the system generates an alarm that would wake the driver up and flash drowsiness alert messages.
