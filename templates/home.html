Reason for this solution

Crashes caused by Fatigue

Within the Southern Region of Australia, fatigue crashes accounted for 11% of all fatal crashes between 1996 and 1998. This is higher compared to drunk driving crashes.

Fatigue Crashes based on Age.

In a study of age groups of truck drivers, the group for 21-25 years made up about 35% of the total Fatigue related accidents. Age group 40-49 years made up about 20%. A trend of steady decline has been noticed as age goes above 49years.

Driving Hours

On average truck driver spend 71.6 hours per week on driving, loading and maintenance. Compared to other motorist this is 4 times more.

Truck drivers work

In a ranking of job satisfaction, 29.2% of Truck drivers major dissatisfaction stemmed from long hours/difficulty hours as first on the list followed by loading and tax.

Truck Stops and Weighbridge in Victoria

Truck drivers can face fatigue anytime during their journey. The map provides a rudimentary idea of where all the rest stops, petrol stations and weighbridge are in Victoria, so that truck drivers can rest to avoid accidents due to weariness.

It also provides the location of a weighbridge for truck drivers along the road, just an additional feature to help them along the journey.

The icon represents what type of location it is on the map; if you hover over it you can find each stops name, address and coordinates to help the truck driver find that location easily.

Note: Only truck friendly rest areas are represented on map.
