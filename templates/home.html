Reason for this solution

Crashes caused by Fatigue

Within the Southern Region, fatigue crashes accounted for 11% of all crashes between 1996 and 1998. This is higher compared to drink driving crashes Fatigue crashes represent 11% of fatal and injury crashes.

Fatigue Crashes based on Age.

Driver age groups involved in fatigue related crashes. Age between 21-25 years has acquired around 35% and 40-49 years around 20% of the age groups. This descreases as we go above 49 years.

Driving Hours

On av erage truck driver spent 71.6 hours per week on driving, loading and maintenance. Compared to other motorist this is 4 times more.

Truck drivers work

Among things they liked least about the job 29.2% Truck drivers put long hours/difficulty hours as first on the list followed by loading and tax.

Rest Stop in Victoria

Truck drivers can face fatigue anytime during their journey. The rest stop map provides a rudimentary idea of where all the truck stops are in Victoria, so that truck drivers can rest to avoid accidents due to weariness.

The map explains where are all the rest stop which has access to trucks, which means a truck can drive up to that place. Red means no access, blue means yes there is a truck access.

The number shown on the map states how many truck parking spaces are there on that rest stop but note that these parking areas are not live, it just tells the overall number of parking space for a truck.
